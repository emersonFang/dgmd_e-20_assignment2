About Emerson Fang

Emerson Fang has played violin for a bajillion years. He is a seasoned piano player, and has played in Timbuktu, the Bermuda Triangle, and Atlantis. Emerson has played on the Moon, Mars, Jupiter, and the surface of the Sun. The surface of the Sun was so hot that he became an instant Super Nova Star. Emerson also has had the opportunity to collaborate with famou Alpine Horn player Jeremy Jeremy, throat singer Mimi Momo, and celebrated Chinese opera performer Fang Fang Fang-Fang. It is with great pleasure that Mr. Fang was awarded the Musician's Eternal Sonata Award while he was visiting the International Space Station. Emerson also has dabbled in timpani, triangle, erhu, guitar, banjo, kazoo, glass playing, banana gun, pots and pans, viola da gamba, and double bass. In addition to his piano gigs, Emerson also is a skilled player piano player.
